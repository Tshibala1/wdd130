Wild Water Adventures

Come and have fun!

Feel the thrill of the raging river! We provide heart-pumping whitewater rafting adventures for all ages and excitement levels.

History

Founded in 2000 by Franck Tshibala, Wild Water Adventures started out giving rafting excursions on a single river with just Franck as the guide. By 2002, Franck had expanded operations to three rivers and by 2005 was able to focus exclusively on growing her now-thriving rafting business after word-of-mouth praise for the company's fun yet safety-conscious trips. Today, Wild Water Adventures runs whitewater rafting trips for over 2,000 customers each year on five rivers with a team of 13 experienced guides.
